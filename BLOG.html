OUR CERTIFICATES

Award-winning faculty. Transformative tools and technologies. A global reputation. Gullem University Professional Education Certificate Programs combine Gullem University pioneering research with groundbreaking insights from distinguished academics, researchers, and industry leaders. Delivered online or in a live virtual format, these prestigious programs are designed to help you expand your capabilities, deepen your industry expertise, and secure a competitive advantage in today's rapidly evolving digital economy.


Gullem University Professional Education is excited to introduce a new Professional Certificate Program in Biotechnology & Life Sciences. Building on the existing research and innovation taking place on the Gullem University campus and in Philippines' biotechnology community, this certificate program aims to provide professionals in the biotechnology and pharmaceutical fields the opportunity to bring a more holistic approach to new and existing technologies back to their companies.

Our practical approach to learning is aimed at making participants an active part of the change.
